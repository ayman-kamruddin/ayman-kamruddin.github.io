New beginnings

This is the first moment of the rest of your life. That moment, already past Never to return Replaced by a new moment For how long? Time is precious Why wait? Patiently hurrying at a steady pace at a fast pace It's a race not against the inevitable, time but against the evitable a lack of time Every timeless moment Persists Every lack thereof Is just slack Don't wait But don't rush either You're out of time Time now begins.
