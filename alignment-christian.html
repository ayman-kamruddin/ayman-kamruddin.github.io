Ayman bin Kamruddin

Tha Alignment Problem - Book Review

An excellent, thoroughly researched review concerning the issues facing Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) today, and some of the potential fixes for tomorrow. Issues limited not just to technical limitations, but more importantly, to social issues such as with hiring, justice, policing, health, and many more. This book provides not only great insights into the functioning of the black-boxes that are ML models, but also provides insights into how humans learn. Touches on philosophy, psychology and neuroscience as much as it touches on computer science and engineering.
