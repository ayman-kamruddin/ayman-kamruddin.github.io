Ayman bin Kamruddin

Hermeneutics - Book Review

This book, as the title indicates, talks about interpretation. It argues that everything is a matter of interpretation, and that without an angle of interpretation, it would be impossible to understand anything. It discusses Heidegger and Derrida, amongst other philosophers, and controversies and ideas surrounding them. Without giving much away, my interpretation is that it takes a post-modern relativist approach to modern hermeneutics.
