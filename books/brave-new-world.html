Ayman bin Kamruddin

Brave New World - Book Review

This book takes psychological and physical conditioning to its logical extreme. A humorous-at-first presentation of a society that has its different echelons hard-coded and generated, with emotional control through a drug called Soma, this book explores a dystopian future where it takes the contemporary classification of society to an extreme. The segment of humanity that lives outside of this conditioning has been relegated to the borderlines of this society, and the protagonist faces a clash between the two.
